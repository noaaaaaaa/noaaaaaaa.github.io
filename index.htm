About

I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Environmental Engineering at University of Queensland, working on wastewater treatment. My research interests lie specifically in the areas of bioinformatics, microbiology and analytical chemistry. I am currently working on building novel nitrogen removal from wastewater over knowledge base and text.

Before coming to UQ, I received my bachelor degree from Zhejiang University, China in 2019.

My supervisors are a team consists of Prof. Zhiguo Yuan, Prof. Jianhua Guo, Dr. Min Zheng and Dr. Ji Lu.
