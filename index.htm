About Me

I am a third-year Ph.D. student in Environmental Engineering at University of Queensland, working on wastewater treatment. My research interests lie specifically in the areas of bioinformatics, microbiology and analytical chemistry. I am currently working on environmental microbiology over knowledge base and text.

Before coming to UQ, I received my bachelor degree from Zhejiang University, China in 2019. I spent a great semester at the Institute of Urban Environment, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS) under the supervision of Dr. Yue Zheng.

My supervisors are a team including Prof. Jianhua Guo, Dr. Min Zheng and Dr. Tao Liu.
